About Hydrangeas


Hydrangeas are beautiful. They are native to Asia and America. They bloom from early spring to late autumn.


Colors

Mostly, the flowers are white, but they can be diffeent colors based upon the soil pH.

  • Pink
  • Red
  • Blue
  • White
  • Light Purple
  • Dark Purple
